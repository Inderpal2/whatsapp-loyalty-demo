🧍 Loyalty Program Journey (Dubai)

🛒 Step 1: Visit the Store

Customer visits a café, salon, gym, or shop offering the loyalty program.

📸 Step 2: See a Sign at the Counter

They see a poster: "Earn Rewards! Scan to Join!"

📲 Step 3: Scan the QR Code

Customer opens phone camera → scans QR → opens WhatsApp chat.

💬 Step 4: Join the Program via WhatsApp

WhatsApp bot welcomes them and asks to upload receipt photo or code.

🎁 Step 5: Earn Points

Receipt is verified, customer gets a WhatsApp message: "✅ You've earned 50 points!"

🎯 Step 6: Redeem Points Later

Customer types "My Rewards" in WhatsApp to see and redeem rewards.

🏪 Store Owner Journey

🛠️ Step 1: Set Up

Owner signs up, gets QR code, prints it, and displays it in-store.

📲 Step 2: Daily Operations

Customers scan and submit receipts. Owner approves points via WhatsApp or mini-dashboard.

🎯 Step 3: Promotions and Offers

Owner sends "Double Points Friday!" promos via WhatsApp to loyal customers.
